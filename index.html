About Me

I am Rohit Kumar Singla, a Vanier Scholar and an MD/PhD candidate at the University of British Columbia (UBC). Entering my seventh and final year, I have defended my PhD in AI-enhanced diagnostics in kidney ultrasound under the expert guidance of Prof. Robert Rohling and Dr. Chris Nguan.

My Work

  • Bloom Burton & Co.: Contract Scientific Consultant since April 2024.
  • Amplitude Ventures: Contract Consultant since November 2023, after completing the Pre-Amp Fellowship in 2022 .
  • Ultrasound Technology Consulting: Working with an undisclosed company to advance ultrasound technology applications.

My research has led to contributions in ultrasound, artificial intelligence, augmented reality, and medical education. Some notable publications include:

  • "Intra‐operative ultrasound‐based augmented reality guidance for laparoscopic surgery" (Healthcare technology letters, Oct 2017) - Introduced an AR system to improve surgical accuracy and safety.
  • "Insights from teaching artificial intelligence to medical students in Canada" (Communications medicine, June 2022) - Explores AI education for medical students to enhance clinical decision-making.
  • "Speckle and shadows: ultrasound-specific physics-based data augmentation for kidney segmentation" (International Conference on Medical Imaging with Deep Learning, Dec 2022) - Describes advanced data augmentation techniques to improve ultrasound image analysis.
